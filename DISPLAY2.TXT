6 digit 7-segment display with Multiplexer controller circuitry:

Date: 01/03/93.
ICs: Disrete TTL. 7447 7-segment font.
Wiring:
                Color   Function

                Green   Ground
                Blue    D0  \
                Purple  D3   \ These are data lines
                Grey    D1   /
                White   D2  /
                Black   A2  \
                Brown   A1   > These lines select the display digit (1 of 6)
                Red     A0  /
                Orange  +5v    Power Supply Input
